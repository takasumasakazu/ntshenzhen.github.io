### Visual SG 2016 at Science Centre Singapore {#1284 .graf .graf--h3 .graf--leading .graf--title name="1284"}

Visual SG is a data visualization and VR event, a bit similar theme of
SIGGRAPH.

The first day 22/June, we do the public reciter whole of the day, over
10 international speakers talk about the theme.

I recorded all video at the front row, but some speech couldn't upload
by copyright policy on YouTube.
