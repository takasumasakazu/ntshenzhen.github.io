### Videos:Shenzhen High Tour for Makers Vol.7 Apr/2017 {#7231 .graf .graf--h3 .graf--leading .graf--title name="7231"}

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*fAaxKmbd6xtVkPDT2HnIww.jpeg){.graf-image}

We saw the Hardware capital Shenzhen city by High tour for makers. That
tour are organized Seeed, Chaihuo, and TAKASU, they are Maker Faire
Shenzhen organizers.\
Our tour is introducing a whole of the Shenzhen ecosystem for makers.
Including Zero to One, One to Many, Idea to Product by Protyping.\
Shenzhen is most high-speed growing city of the world, I want to make
the world to be better much faster such as there by technology and
imagine.\
We got to have a really good time together with Shenzhen makers, we
share these amazing talks for world makers.
