Why DIY Pop music movement born and rise in Japan?

HATSUNE Miku and other voice synthesizer chalactors are famous in the
world. Most one HATSUNE Miku selected by Google theme song singer, with
Lady Gaga. 

In fact, they are just synthesized vocal softwares, Whole of them music
is made by musician,mostly DIY music hobbyist. These are over thousands
professional quality tune born from uncountable number of DIY tune. 

And every famous DIY tune including beautiful video,made by different
people's collaboration between Tunes maker and Movie maker.

A DIY music community that does do everything of music labels, include
produce,promotion, research and development, networking,,etc. That
something similar Open Source Software community. 

This document try to express DIY music community in Japan, and think
what similar Open Source Software.
