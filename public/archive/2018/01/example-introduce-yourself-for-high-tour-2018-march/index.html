Example: Introduce yourself for Shenzhen High Tour 2018 March#

You have to write up blog article about introducing yourself for application Shenzhen High Tour, in English or in Japanese.
This article is for introduceing other member, not for me. We are community.

This is an example. (In Japanese is Here)

**1.Self introduction
**I`m Takasu, organizer of this High Tour. And also I support to some Maker Faire, Shenzhen, Singapore, Chengdu and Xian. I was the most experienced person of the world about Maker Faire in Asia.
 I`m living in Shenzhen from last Dec. I`m loving my maker friend in Shenzhen, I can`t wait to introduce them to yours.

**2.My job,How I get own money 
**I`m working in Switch Science{.markup–anchor .markup–p-anchor}, they are maker tools maker and distributor similar as Seeed. My role is global business development. That is the reason why I`m living in Shenzhen now.
And I often write up report about Makers, in Japanease and English.

3.My Maker Project, How I make or do on your time 
Support and visit Maker Faire is the most my hobby. And also I organizer of Dance party for Geeks, called AkiParty. The Shenzhen High tour is also not my job, this tour is Volunteer works. But these community outreach is just between my hobby and Business.
I often make own homebrew beer when I live in Singapore, until last year.

**4.How help other people in this trip 
**I can use the Didi (Taxi App) and some apps in Chinese, and Speak English and bit Chinese. But I guess I`m quite busy in the tour for organizing, You should find other people.
