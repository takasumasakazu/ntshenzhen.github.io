Nothing that can not be made here Shenzhen supply chain

This time they participated in a tour to guide the supply chain to the startup, and I saw Shenzhen being called a world factory. I saw seven different factories in 3 days, but the following points are common.

· Every manufacturer has world-class level design, production and problem solving skills
· Flexible and quick response
· Higher level of confidentiality, such as checking belongings by whole body counter at entrance of factory
· Contracts can be tied in English
· Perhaps the price range is higher in Shenzhen, but there is no feeling that cost is spent on unnecessary places and high price with no brand name is added with brand name
· Current business partners are lined with famous manufacturers including famous companies in Japan, Europe and the US

Since it is obvious in Shenzhen that it is early / cheap to some extent, I felt that it is characterized by being reliable in terms of quality and delivery time, and being able to respond flexibly to ambiguous orders of startups. SKY Prototype 1 to 1000

SkyPrototype is a milling company. In trial production in Chinese, it often refers to “making one by one” with cutting and 3D printers, rather than “trying on a trial.” Injection molding commonly used in mass production first uses a lot of money to make a “mold” and pours plastic into it to make the exterior. It is cheap enough to make as much as possible, but the initial cost is necessary, once you make the mold, it is difficult for you to fix it, it is not suitable for use such as “make only 10 pieces and try to exhibit at the exhibition”.

Process 3D printers and cutting processes one by one.
Even if one or even 1000, the cost per one will not change, initial investment will not be applied. Even if it finally mass-produces and lines up in a mass merchandiser, even if it is necessary for exhibitions and cloud funding, use cutting work and gather feedback several times until the mistake is not made, It is a good choice to raise money for investment.

SkyPrototype performs small lot processing with 1 to several thousands as a catch phrase, and many engineers ride in consultation. If you inform us of the exterior CAD data and application, the necessary number etc., we will consider the material, processing method, finishing method etc. together. Since that consulting service is an incidental work of manufacturing, it is free, communication is also roughly in English. The factory has machines and craftsmen who can do various processes for that purpose, and exchange is said to start from “sending prototype first”.

Of course, not only prototype but SkyPrototype is handling products, such as finished products of automobile gear boxes, which require accuracy and material that CNC can not deliver even if the cost rises somewhat. First of all, there are a lot of representative machines for small lot production and various CNCs. A large number of CNCs with different roles, such as machines capable of complicated machining such as gouging, large machines capable of producing large parts, such as 4 axes and 5 axes, line up.

Invox/Sun On Synbol of Manucatcurling

Injection molding, making metal exteriors in large quantities using metal molds is a synonym for mass production. Many of fabcross’s readers will recognize that the mold is as expensive and valuable as to be an asset.
Sun On Group of Dongguan City is its injection molding factory. Founded in 1997, currently counts 1,000 employees and has sold $ 45 million. We produce and output 150 to 200 project dies per month, 30% of whom are foreign clients.

{.graf-image}

The difficult point for injection molding is that it can not be fixed at a later time.
Sun On Group sells proposal power by preparing engineers who are familiar with all the manufacturing processes and repeating prototyping before raising the mold. For example, when undertaking injection molding of a water dispenser, output a prototype using a 3D printer that can output large items, and verify that “it can be properly used in this form and water flows”. The 3D CAD data is shared with the client, and of course you can send prototypes by mail.
An estimate including the prototype cost is presented at the initial exchange of “what to use for what kind of requirement specification” and the interaction on the way is included in the final output fee.

{.graf-image}

The black part of the exterior of the cell phone in this picture is made of injection molded plastic. It is necessary to fasten the screws, but cutting the screw threads to plastic becomes uneasy in terms of strength.
Sun On proposed a structure fitting a threaded metal nut and made it “work well” from all aspects of strength, cost and ease of manufacture. Since this is inside it does not matter that the texture and color are different.
In order to solve various problems on Sun On, there are not only injection molding but also all molding lines including aluminum casting and pressing.

EMTEK Certification

The difference between the products that line up in the store and the prototype that startup makes is the quality including safety. To line up with mass merchandising stores, it is necessary to ensure security including certification by the certification body of each country. EMTEK provides comprehensive inspection / Certification services.

Founded in 2000, EMTEK currently has annual sales of $ 24.6 million annually, and 5 to 6 new products are handled monthly. In famous places, Takara Tomy’s toys have been inspected here, which seems to be a 15% cost savings compared to the former vendor. In addition, it is a big company with nineteen support offices around the world including inspection of the new model BB — 8 of the robot toy used to connect with the smartphone Sphero here.

{.graf-image}

{.graf-image}

In EMTEK, FCC, CE and technical suitability can be taken at one factory, but this is also consulting here as well, considering the optimum plan by product and budget. Those that are closely attached to the skin like a smartphone and loaded with various wireless equipments require a variety of tests, which is said to be a case of a smart phone development in the United States, it took only 60,000 dollars for the inspection process. What kind of inspection is done is the know-how of EMTEK, the more expensive the inspection is, the more expensive it is, but the problem is discovered later and the risk of product recall is lowered. It is one of the values ​​of EMTEK to propose inspection plans by stepping into what kind of products and what users use.

The time to create a test plan and end the test is on average 3 to 4 weeks. There are also premises to break the product during the test, so if you have about 3 to 4 samples, it will be over in that period, but with only one there seems to be more time.

TeamGiant (Battely) and Stephen Gould(Packaging and Shipment)

In Shenzhen, packing service companies that undertake final stages of manufacturing, companies that make batteries themselves from scratch are all within the range that any service necessary for the manufacturing industry can be accessed.

Shenzhen’s battery maker teamGiant is a company that can process raw materials and make battery cells themselves. In addition to batteries that can withstand approximately 500 charging used in general electronic devices such as smart phones, they also manufacture high-life batteries that can withstand over 2000 charges used in electric vehicles and the like.

The factory has been almost automated, it is possible to observe but the inside can not be taken. We also customize automation monitoring system for our company. The company’s strength is the high performance of the basic battery cell, which is created by hiring a lot of doctors and masters, and the quality of testing the safety by leaving the completed battery charged for 48 hours for safety. At the time of the test, 2000 charging cycles are done in-house, and the barcode attached to the battery of the finished product contains not only the date of manufacture and lot, but also information on where the material was purchased , And devised so that quality can be further improved when there is a problem. If you just paste labels etc. using existing battery cells, if you are going to adjust the capacity, voltage, size etc from small hundreds of small lot and make a completely custom battery, assuming a capacity of 5000 mA, It can be developed from about a piece. If the capacity is bigger like those used for electric cars, the number is small, and as the capacity is small like the one put in the action camera, the number increases.

I saw whole of process in here Shenzhen, from Prototype to Product

The 3 days I experienced this time was a tour to see the accumulation of enormous manufacturing industry accumulated from all over the world in the last 30 years.

In Shenzhen you can receive all services from prototype to shipment in a place where you can hear. It seemed that they were motivated to commercialize “the work which is not in the world, the first work” which was able to be able to talk in English in all the visited companies and it was flexible. Regardless of a standalone dealer, its accumulation is only in Shenzhen. Also, HWTrek will not introduce it, but if you search by yourself, you will find lots of cheaper suppliers. I think that scalability is also the result of accumulation.

The integration increases the quality and the cost decreases. The factory operation cost in the Shenzhen area has been rising year by year, but it seems that the merit of progressing accumulation has been won for a while for a while. In terms of individual vendors, there are many places where they are considering relocating to Xiamen (Amoy) or Malaysia. Shenzhen is also a location that can be their hub.

In fact, many of the factories are no longer “Shenzhen City” but are moving to neighboring Dongguan City, etc. The center of Shenzhen City has become a city with higher added value such as financial district and service. If you are in Dongguan you can do the same thing as Shenzhen, it is another challenging goal whether to have the same quality when you move the factory to Xiamen and even overseas. Japanese companies have many such experiences, but Chinese companies will experience it from now.

Nonetheless, the future will be more globalized. It will be more urbanized and more merit will come out. The role will change, but it seems that it will continue to be in the place where “for the time being let’s go to Shenzhen” for startup for a while.
